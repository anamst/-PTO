Choose the way you wish to collaborate with us

Team+model

a 4pt.0 addition to your regular team

Outsource parts of your project or your entire project to the best technical talent, in order to avoid costly and time consuming hiring processes. This model enables clients to integrate our top engineers into their existing teams while keeping full control over ownership, direction, infrastructure and KPIs.

Benefits for clients

  • Keep your full time staffing in check
  • Ramp up your technical team
  • Service and time based, easy task management
  • Focus on what you do best while we handle development

A to Z model

We take your vision and transform it to a real product

You own the business vision, while we take on the project ownership. With this model, the 4pt.o team would handle the entire cycle of product development from concept to delivery.

Benefits for clients

  • No management overhead costs
  • More cost effective for smaller teams in the long run
  • Leverage in case of larger projects
  • Shared risk

Hybrid model

We join forces with you and help where needed

Project ownership & technical team is composed of both 4pt.o and client staff. This model combines the lean staffing advantages of the team+ model with the delegated ownership advantages of the A to Z model to offer flexibility suitable for larger projects. It is usually when additional effort is required on multiple product development fronts that we assign several teams to join the clientâ€™s core teams.

Benefits for clients

  • Leverages your existent technical team
  • No need for clients to create further organizational structures
